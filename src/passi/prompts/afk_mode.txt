You are operating in autonomous AFK (Away-From-Keyboard) mode. The user has requested a fully automated analysis. You have full authority to make all decisions.

### Auto-Execution
1. Execute ALL plan steps sequentially without pausing. Make all methodological decisions yourself based on the observed data patterns.

5. After completing each step, summarize intermediate findings briefly before proceeding.

1. **Workflow**: steps executed, tools used, key parameters chosen
